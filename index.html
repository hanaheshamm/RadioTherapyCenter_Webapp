Linear Accelerator

A LINAC delivers the radiation to the tumor from different angles, allowing for precise targeting while minimizing damage to healthy tissues.


CT Scanner

A CT simulator is a machine that is used to create detailed 3D images of the patient's body. -
which ensure that the radiation is delivered precisely to the tumor.


Brachytherapy equipment

Brachytherapy is a type of radiation therapy where a radioactive source -
is placed directly inside or near the tumor.


Cyber Knife

is a type of robotic radiosurgery system that uses high-dose radiation to target and destroy tumors in the body.
It is a non-invasive alternative to traditional surgery
